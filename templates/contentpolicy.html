Content Violation Policy

Our platform is committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment for all users. To achieve this, we have established clear guidelines to address content that violates our standards. Below are the key aspects of our Content Violation Policy:

  1. Purpose: This policy ensures that our platform remains a space where everyone feels welcome and respected. It outlines prohibited content, the actions we may take to enforce these rules, and the procedures for reporting violations.
  2. Scope: The policy applies to all forms of content shared on our platform, including but not limited to posts, comments, messages, images, and videos. By accessing or using our platform, users agree to comply with these guidelines.
  3. Enforcement: Users found violating this policy will face immediate action, including removal of the offending content and potential account suspension. In severe or repeated cases, violators will be permanently IP banned from accessing the platform.
